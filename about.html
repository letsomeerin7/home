About Me

Hi friends, I’m thrilled you’re here! I’m Erin Jamison and this is the online home for Let Some Erin. So, what are we doing here? I’m glad you asked.

Pretty much my entire life I’ve struggled with confidence, self-esteem, and identity. I didn’t know who I am or what I want or how worthy I am of all the love and good things in life. This last year, though, I’ve experienced some real breakthroughs and have learned to recognize my inherent value, my needs, and my goals.

I feel like my whole life I’ve been asleep, letting life happen around me while dreaming about what it could be. This last year I opened my eyes and woke up. Now, it’s time to start the day and start LIVING.

That being said, I by no means have all the answers - I’m still going through my journey, and really, I’ve just gotten started! But I want to share the things I’m learning in the hopes that you’ll join me in this transformation, which is how this podcast came to be. So that we can go through it together and come out better than we ever imagined or thought possible.

“I find the best way to love someone is not to change them, but instead, help them reveal the greatest version of themselves.”

-Steve Maraboli
